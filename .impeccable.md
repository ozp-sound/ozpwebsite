## Design Context

### Users
Primary visitors are listeners first, collaborators second, and recruiters third.
Visitors are checking ozpwebsite's music/software portfolio.
They need to quickly understand identity, style, and representative work, then jump to external platforms (Bilibili/X) with minimal friction.

### Brand Personality
Minimal, futuristic, expressive.
Tone should feel emotional and artistic while still precise and controlled.
The experience should keep a strong personal signature instead of template-like portfolio patterns.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode first, with blue-gray-white palette and restrained tech texture.
Visual language: clean typography, soft glass cards, subtle motion, low-noise background effects, and emotion-forward artistic atmosphere.
Avoid heavy navigation chrome, text-heavy explanations, and crowded UI.

### Design Principles
- Prioritize immediate identity recognition on first screen.
- Keep interface minimal: show only essential information and actions.
- Use motion as atmosphere, not distraction.
- Preserve visual consistency through shared spacing, radius, and color tokens.
- Favor scannable, card-based content blocks with clear click targets.
